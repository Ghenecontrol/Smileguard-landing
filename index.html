How it works

Three steps to better health decisions.

No waiting rooms, no travel costs. Get trusted health guidance from wherever you are, in minutes.

01
Describe your symptoms
Simply tell SmileGuard how you're feeling — in plain language, your native language, or using voice. No medical jargon required.
02
AI analyzes & consults
Our trained AI model evaluates your symptoms against a validated medical knowledge base and generates a tailored assessment in seconds.
03
Receive your care plan
Get a personalized treatment plan — home remedies, medication guidance, lifestyle tips — plus clear signals for when to seek professional care.
Platform features

Built for real people
in real situations.

SmileGuard is designed from the ground up for accessibility, accuracy, and compassion.

Individual-tailored plans
Every consultation is unique. SmileGuard factors in your age, medical history, and current symptoms to generate a plan that fits you — not a generic template.
24/7 availability
Health concerns don't follow business hours. SmileGuard is always on — at 2am, weekends, and public holidays — for communities that need it most.
Smart escalation — know when to see a doctor
Our AI is trained to recognize danger signs. When symptoms suggest something beyond home management, SmileGuard clearly tells users to seek professional care — and helps them find the nearest appropriate facility.
